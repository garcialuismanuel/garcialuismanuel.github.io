‌Breweries by States

Colorado has highest number of Breweries and North Dakota is lowest among 50

states and Washington DC.

‌Median ABV by States

The median ABV values are from 0.04 to 0.06 over the states.

‌Median IBU by States

The median IBU is from 20 to 60 over the states. There is more variance for IBU.


‌Maximum ABV and IBU

Colorado has the maximum ABV (0.128).

Oregon has the maximum IBU (138).

‌Summary Statistics

The ABV variable has a mode around .5, is right skewed, and has a range of

about .11.


‌Relationship between ABV and IBU

This data shows mild positive linear relationship(3rd degree polynomial) between

ABV and IBU, even though most of beer bitterness comes from hops.

We have analyzed the alcohol contents(ABV) and bitterness(IBU) of craft beers in

the breweries of United States.

IBU values and verified with beer samples in the market including Budweiser.
